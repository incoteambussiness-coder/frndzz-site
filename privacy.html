01

Data Collection

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • check_circle
    Account Information: When you sign up, we ask for personal information, like your name, email address, and phone number to store with your account.
  • check_circle
    Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with our services, such as the content you view and the features you use.
  • check_circle
    Device Information: Information about the device you're using, including model, OS version, and unique device identifiers.
02

Purpose of Processing

Inco Team Technology processes your data based on legitimate business interests and to fulfill our service commitments to you:

Service Delivery

To maintain and provide the core Frndzz social experience and messaging features.

Security & Safety

Monitoring for fraudulent activity, harassment, and unauthorized access to protect our community.

Product Improvement

Analyzing how users navigate Frndzz to build better, more intuitive social features.

Communication

Sending service updates, security alerts, and administrative messages regarding your account.

03

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with companies, organizations, or individuals outside of Inco Team Technology except in the following cases:

  • With your consent: We will share personal information outside of Frndzz when we have your explicit consent.
  • Third-party Gateways: We use trusted payment processors and cloud infrastructure providers (AWS, Stripe) that comply with strict privacy standards.
  • Legal requirements: We will share information if we have a good-faith belief that access, use, preservation, or disclosure is necessary to meet any applicable law.
"Frndzz utilizes state-of-the-art encryption during transit to ensure that any data shared with processing partners remains inaccessible to unauthorized third parties."
04

User Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have specific rights regarding your personal data:

Access & Portability expand_more

Rectification & Deletion expand_more
You have the right to correct inaccurate data or request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data.
Right to Object expand_more
You can object to our processing of your data for marketing or profiling purposes at any time.
