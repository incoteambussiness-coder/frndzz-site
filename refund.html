Overview

At Frndzz, we strive to provide a seamless social experience. This Refund Policy outlines the terms under which refunds may be issued for digital goods, subscriptions, and virtual currency ("Credits") purchased within the Frndzz platform. By making a purchase, you agree to these terms.

lock_person

1. Final Sales

All purchases of virtual items, digital credits, and premium subscriptions on Frndzz are considered Final and Non-Refundable except as expressly required by local consumer law or specifically stated in Section 2.

Reasoning: Digital assets are consumed immediately upon delivery to your account, and cannot be "returned" in the traditional sense.

priority_high

2. Refund Exceptions

settings Technical Errors

If a technical glitch causes a purchase to fail while you were still charged, or if digital assets were not delivered to your inventory.

payments Duplicate Charges

Instances where our payment system erroneously charges your method of payment multiple times for a single transaction.

Note: Unauthorized purchases made by minors or third parties are subject to the investigation policies of the respective App Store (Apple) or Play Store (Google).

3. Request Process

To initiate a refund request, you must follow these steps within 48 hours of the transaction:

1

Gather your transaction ID (found in your email receipt or Apple/Google billing history).

2

Contact our support team via the 'Help' section in the Frndzz app or email support@incoteam.tech.

3

Provide a clear description of the issue and attach any relevant screenshots of error messages.

schedule

4. Processing Timeline

Internal Review

24-48 Business Hours

Bank Processing

5-7 Business Days

Once approved, the refund is issued back to the original payment method. Please note that banking cycles vary by institution. International cards may take up to 10 days to reflect the credit.
